FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Is my data really private? +

Yes. RegSecure AI runs entirely in your browser. Your documents are never uploaded to any server. We literally cannot see your data—there's no server to send it to.

Does it work offline? +

Yes, after the initial AI model download (~1.5GB, one-time). Progressive processing with persistent local state means you can resume work instantly—even after browser restarts or losing connection.

What browsers are supported? +

Chrome, Edge, and Safari (macOS) with WebGPU support. Firefox support coming soon. Desktop recommended for best performance.

Is it accurate enough for legal work? +

RegSecure AI uses paragraph-aware segmentation for higher accuracy on complex legal documents. It's designed to assist, not replace, legal review—it flags potential risks and missing clauses, but always have a qualified attorney review important contracts.

How do I clear my local data? +

You can clear all encrypted data at any time through the settings menu. Additionally, you can set an emergency password that instantly wipes all local traces from the machine—zero forensic footprint.

What's the pricing after beta? +

Beta is free. Post-launch pricing will be flat per-seat (no usage fees or per-token charges). Early adopters will get locked-in pricing. Subscribe below to stay updated.
